Writing a great title

The title of your AMA should grab redditors attention and briefly describe who you are. You can mention a notable accomplishment, an upcoming event that you are involved in, or a free giveaway.

More Tips:

  • You cannot edit the title after you submit your AMA. Proofread it multiple times before submitting.
  • Do not promote anything in your title unless you are giving away something for free or announcing something that the audience cares deeply about.

Writing a great body

This is the additional content/information that subscribers will see once they enter the thread. Include promotional information, links to official websites, more details about your music/background, etc. This section can be edited at a later date as well, i.e. when the talent has to leave they can say, "Edit: This has been great. Thanks for all the questions!"

Note: Please don't use link shorteners here, because the filter hates them and will probably remove your post. If you subsequently edit the post to include a link shortener, the filter will automatically remove it, so please avoid them altogether!

Creating a verification link

You must include verification with your AMA in the form of a link. Here are some good examples of proof you can provide:

  • A tweet from your official account tagging the ListenToThis twitter (@L2This).
  • A Facebook post from your official account tagging the ListenToThis Facebook page.
  • An email to our address (redditlistentothis@gmail.com) from an account that appears on one of your official artist pages or website.
  • A link to a photo of you with a sign that says "Hi Reddit" with your username.

Note: This field is required. If you verified via email, enter a link to your Twitter or Facebook account.
